Built from Gabriel's kitchen experiments.

881 Grab & Go mixes Jamaican comfort food with the bold, fast-paced sandwich style Gabriel discovered in Philadelphia. By experimenting with sauces, breads, fillings, and loaded sandwich builds, he created a fresh take on island food made for Hartford.

From oxtail plates and jerk chicken to grab-and-go sandwiches packed with Jamaican flavor and street-style energy, the menu is built to feel familiar, new, and easy to crave.
